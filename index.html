About Me

Hello! I’m Samuel Ochoche Peter, a Computer Science graduate from the Federal University of Kashere. I specialize in both front-end and back-end development, with a passion for creating dynamic web applications that provide seamless user experiences.

Here are a few projects I’ve successfully developed:

  • Smartphone Tracking System: A platform that allows users to report lost smartphones and block their bank accounts in real-time.
  • Online Pharmacy: A web-based system where customers can register, purchase medications, and book appointments with medical professionals.
  • Online Clearance System: An automated system for student clearance processes, integrating notifications between departments.
  • Online Vehicle Management System: A platform for tracking vehicle availability and maintenance.

My expertise includes:

  • Web Development: Proficient in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PHP, and MySQL, enabling robust full-stack applications.
  • Programming: Skilled in Python, particularly in data analysis and machine learning.
  • Teaching: Experience in sharing my knowledge of Python programming with others.

I thrive on leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and am always eager to learn and grow in the ever-evolving tech landscape. When I’m not coding, you can find me reading or exploring new ideas in tech.

I look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals and collaborating on projects that make a positive impact!
